# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and safe environment for everyone.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Be mindful of different perspectives and experiences.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.

The following behavior is unacceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or deliberate disruption.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels).

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:

- polribasrovira@gmail.com

Reports will be handled with care and discretion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings or removal from the community.
